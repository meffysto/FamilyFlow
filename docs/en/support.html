Family Vault — Help & Contact

Family Vault is a free app, with no ads and no subscription. Support is provided on a voluntary basis.

Frequently asked questions

  • Where is my data stored? — On your device, in plain text files. Nothing is sent online unless you enable the optional AI feature.
  • How do I sync between devices? — Place your data folder in iCloud Drive.
  • I lost my data — Make sure your folder is in iCloud Drive. Your files are readable with any text editor.
  • Face ID / PIN is not working — Go to Settings > Security to reset the protection.
  • Widgets show nothing — Open the app once to initialize the shared data with widgets.
