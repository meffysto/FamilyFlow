STYLE (préambule à coller / réutiliser pour CHAQUE meuble) :
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Hand-painted top-down RPG furniture asset for a cozy game, matching a warm
illustrated wooden room interior (Stardew Valley HD vibe, painterly, NOT pixel
art). Slight 3/4 overhead perspective (same angle as a gently tilted floor).
Light from TOP-LEFT, soft shadow to bottom-right + a soft contact shadow on the
ground so it sits in the room. Warm palette: honey wood, cream, soft browns and
greens. Single object, centered, isolated, filling ~70% of frame. TRANSPARENT
background (PNG alpha), no floor, no walls, no text, no border, no watermark.
Square 512x512. Consistent scale (an armchair ≈ 65% of frame height).
