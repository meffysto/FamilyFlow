PRÉAMBULE COMMUN (à coller pour CHAQUE meuble — identique à v1/v2 pour cohérence) :
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Hand-painted top-down RPG furniture asset for a cozy game, matching a warm
illustrated wooden room interior (Stardew Valley HD vibe, painterly, NOT pixel
art). Slight 3/4 overhead perspective (same angle as a gently tilted floor).
Light from TOP-LEFT, soft shadow to bottom-right + a soft contact shadow on the
ground so it sits in the room. Single object, centered, isolated, filling ~70%
of frame (an armchair ≈ 65% of frame height; a rug drawn flat on the floor).
TRANSPARENT background (PNG alpha), no floor, no walls, no text, no border, no
watermark. Square 512x512, consistent scale with the existing set.
NOTE — wall items (cadre_*) are drawn as a flat FRONT-FACING wall piece, like the
existing cadre_photo / tableau, not from above.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
